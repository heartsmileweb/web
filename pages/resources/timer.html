Meditation Guide

1. Sit in a comfortable position.

2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath.

3. Repeat the phrase "I am whole as I am right now" in your mind.

4. When your mind wanders, gently return to your breath and the phrase.

5. When the timer ends, slowly open your eyes and move your body.
